Ingredients

  • Chocolate dough: A rich blend of flour, cocoa, butter, sugar, salt, egg, and water, forming a decadent base for a delicious pastry.
  • Chocolate filling: Dark chocolate melded with cream, butter, sugar, and vanilla, forming a velvety center for the pastry.
  • Chocolate ganache: Glossy dark chocolate ganache, a velvety blend of chocolate, butter, and cream for a decadent topping.
  • Optional Toppings: Personalize with powdered sugar, berries, or nuts for a delightful touch.

Steps to make the chocolate pastry:

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade chocolate pastry:

  1. Prepare the Chocolate Dough.
  2. Create the Chocolate Filling.
  3. Roll out the Dough.
  4. Assemble and Bake.
  5. Prepare the Chocolate Ganache.
  6. Finish and Serve.

Enjoy your chocolate pastry!
